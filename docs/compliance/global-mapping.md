# Global Compliance Mapping

## Standards Covered

- GDPR
- DORA
- NIS2
- PSD3
- PCI-DSS v4.0
- EU AI Act

## Applicability Matrix

| Standard | Applies To | Key Requirement | Planned Control |
|---|---|---|---|
| GDPR | KYC and user profile data | Lawful processing and minimization | Data retention and tokenization policy |
| DORA | Platform operations | ICT risk management and resilience | Monitoring, incident runbooks, IaC |
| NIS2 | Service infrastructure | Security governance and reporting | Threat model and incident workflows |
| PSD3 | Payment services | Strong customer protection | Auth controls and traceable payment events |
| PCI-DSS v4.0 | Card-related processing paths | Secure handling and access controls | Segmentation and secrets vault patterns |
| EU AI Act | AI decisioning components | Risk transparency and governance | Model registry, explainability notes |
